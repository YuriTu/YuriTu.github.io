Who Am I?

Hi everyone! I would like to introduce myself and my work. I am a graphic designer based in Davis, California. I currently a senior at University of California, Davis and have a few years of experience with various projects. I am not only interested in design but many other art forms as well. I have years of experience on black and white Photography, and drawing and painting. I enjoy the creating process of design, and I love design because it is a tool to make life easier and simplier. I would like to get into fashion/lifestyle projects in the near future so as a start I say hi here. There is still a lot to go and evolve but so far here is what I did: I hope you will enjoy my work as much as I do.
